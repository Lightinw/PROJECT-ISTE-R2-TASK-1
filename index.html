EVENT SCHEDULE

8:00 am - 9:00 am: Set up and Registration - Arrange for the necessary equipment, such as colors, water, and hoses. - Set up registration desks or booths for participants to sign in and collect their entry passes or wristbands.

9:00 am - 9:30 am: Welcome and Introduction - Gather all participants in a central area and give a brief introduction to the event. - Welcome everyone and explain the guidelines and rules for playing Holi safely.

9:30 am - 10:30 am: Cultural Performances and Music - Organize cultural performances, such as dance or music, to entertain the participants. - Arrange for a DJ or music system to play Holi-themed songs and keep the energy high.

10:30 am - 11:30 am: Holi Celebrations - Open the Holi festivities, allowing participants to play with colors and water. - Ensure there are designated areas for different activities like dry color, wet color, and water balloons. - Provide a safe environment and ensure participants follow safety

11:30 am - 12:00 pm: Cleanup and Conclusion - Announce the end of the Holi celebrations and request participants to start cleaning up. - Provide bins and trash bags for collecting used colors and other waste. - Thank everyone for their participation and announce any upcoming events or announcements.
